About William:

William has been an active member of the Liberal Party for 30+ years. He has lived in Bowmanville for the last 6 years with his partner Sarah. William has worked across the political and private sector in senior management roles. He has also worked in the public sector at the municipal, provincial and federal level. William worked as Job Developer for Durham College for five years and liaised with hundreds of SMEs to help create jobs for young graduates; surpassing all of his targets. He has also been a business owner and a stay-at-home dad.

He is an active member of the St. John’s Anglican Church in Bowmanville serving in various senior level capacities. He was a volunteer member of the Humane Society of Northumberland, Fundraising and Chair of the Peterborough Public Library. William’s contributions to the community and to the country were recognized as a Recipient of the Commemorative Medal for the 125th Anniversary of Confederation.

William has a Bachelor of Arts degree from Mount Alison University and Katholieke Universiteit Leuven in Belgium. He also has a Mediation certificate from Harvard University.
